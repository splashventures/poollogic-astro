## Decision Log
- Used catch-all route src/pages/california/[...path].astro instead of nested routes for flexibility
- Preserved exact URL slugs from WordPress including inconsistencies (OC flat paths, rancho-sante-fe typo)
- DM Sans from @fontsource instead of Google Fonts for performance
- Server-side GTM proxy at file.cabanapools.com/gtm.js (existing setup)
- Blog posts created with placeholder content; full scraping is a follow-up task
- Text-based logo ("Cabana") used instead of image logo (no logo image provided)

## Surprises & Discoveries
- OC sub-cities have inconsistent URL nesting: Newport Beach under /california/orange-county-pool-service/ but most others flat under /california/
- Sacramento has a duplicate path: /california/sacramento-pool-service/sacramento-sacramento-pool-service/
- Cabana has 2 addresses: SD (1223 Cleveland Ave) and Sacramento (2281 Lava Ridge Court)
- Live site uses different phone numbers by region: (888) 388-9689 for SD/LA/OC, (916) 909-1244 for Sacramento
